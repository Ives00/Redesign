COLOR ME AU-SOME 5K WALK/RUN

Let's get our color on! Each year, we host a Color Me Au-Some 5K Walk/Run. This event is a fun and colorful 5k with a twist; participants are plastered with color bombs of dry tempera paint powder at stations throughout the course.Thank you for participating this year and we look forward to seeing you at our next one!

Ashes to Bricks

Our home facility endured a fire in 2018. The fire left us looking for a new home for these children to keep learning. We recently built a new center in Wheeling. The children once again have their garden and play spaces.

With 8 large classrooms, 8 individual classrooms, a living skills room, a sensory room, and an occupational therapy room to name a few, the learning opportunities are endless for these children. It wouldn't be possible without your kind donations.

Shawn Ramsay Scholarship

This is after Shawn Ramsay who had recently passed. Her family opened a scholarship in her name to help alleviate costs in attending ALLC (many insurances provide little help). Shawn was a case supervisor at our center and will be dearly missed by everyone.
