About Us

MISSION: The Augusta Levy Learning Center aims to enhance the social, academic, communication and independent living skills of its students.

The Augusta Levy Learning Center, located in Wheeling, WV, is the first intensive autism treatment program in the state. Currently overseen by Dr. O. Ivar Lovaas of UCLA and his colleagues, their methods are used by trained behavioral technicians.

Parents

Parents! We have opportunities for you, too! We provide weekly training sessions for parents, have parent workshops every 4-8 weeks, and offer support for coordination of services in the home. We communicate with you daily.

Students

Each Student will receive about 30-40 hours of individual intruction time. They will work with trained behavioral technicians.
